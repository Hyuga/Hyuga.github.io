Meet Milk.

Mofufu Milk is a soft, snow-white kitten from Moyachi Company. With rosy cheeks and big, slightly sleepy round eyes, just having her close makes your heart melt a little. She's a little bundle of kindness.

    Her favorite time is when the sky glows at sunset. Her treat of choice? A tasty little fish.

  • Always by Your Side

    On good days and tough days alike, she's always waiting with a “welcome home.”
